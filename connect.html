Connect sources

Connect only what Silent Sentinel needs to watch. Keep control of everything else.

Silent Sentinel is being built around read-only access. It watches for signals. It does not act for you.

Before any connection, the rules should be clear.

Silent Sentinel will use read-only access wherever possible, avoid raw archives, and store only the signals needed to help you notice what matters.

Email

Missed replies and quiet threads.

Watches for unanswered asks, follow-up gaps, cooling contact patterns, and threads that may need human attention.

Calendar

Meetings, shifts, and gaps.

Watches for repeated cancellations, missing follow-ups after meetings, and dates that matter but have no next action.

Documents

Deadlines hiding in files.

Watches for dates, commitments, changes, and terms that may be overlooked if they stay buried in documents.

What Silent Sentinel may do

  • Read only what is needed to detect useful signals.
  • Create signal cards for overlooked information.
  • Show a plain source trail back to the user’s own tools.
  • Learn from acknowledge and dismiss actions.

What Silent Sentinel will not do

  • It will not send emails.
  • It will not delete, move, or edit anything.
  • It will not change meetings.
  • It will not approve work or make decisions for you.
